Who We Are?

A group of final year students studying in AISSMS, COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING and developed this model as our final year project.

What We Do?

This system will make sure that people follow the rules and regulations imposed by the government such as social distancing and wearing a mask at all times. The system also checks that the person entering does not have high temperature and will dispence sanitiser.

Why This Project ?

We created this system to provide some contribution and help to reduce the spread of corona virus so that people can back to their normal life with little to no risk of getting infected.
